Who We Are

A Proudly Sri Lankan
Travel Company

Leeza Travels is a proudly Sri Lankan travel agency in Colombo. We have spent over a decade building personalised travel experiences for thousands of Sri Lankan and international travellers.

Our team of passionate, experienced travel consultants brings deep local knowledge combined with global expertise. Whether you are dreaming of a family holiday across Srilanka, we make it happen seamlessly.

From the moment you contact us to the day you return home, we are beside you every step of the way (handling hotels, visa assistance, guided tours and 24/7 on-trip support), so you can simply enjoy the journey.

Our Vision

Where We Are Going

Our vision is to become the most trusted and innovative travel company in Srilanka. Connecting Sri Lankans and travellers worldwide to extraordinary destinations through personalised, responsible and inspiring journeys.

We envision a world where exceptional travel is accessible to everyone, shaped by local expertise and delivered with world-class service. We strive to be the first name that comes to mind whenever someone dreams of exploring the Srilanka.

Our Mission

How We Get There

Our mission is to craft unforgettable, personalised travel experiences that exceed expectations at every step. We are committed to delivering seamless journey planning, honest pricing and outstanding customer care from the first inquiry to the safe return home.

We work with handpicked hotels, and experienced local guides to ensure every traveller receives the very best. Our mission also includes promoting sustainable and responsible tourism that supports local communities.
